Attractions

Explore the stunning tourist spots Sorsogon has to offer.

Sorsogon City

Sorsogon Cathedral (Saints Peter and Paul Parish Cathedral)

Address: Rizal Street, Sorsogon City, Sorsogon

Operating Hours: 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Description: Established in 1628, this Roman Catholic cathedral stands as a testament to Sorsogon's rich religious history. Facing Sorsogon Bay, its eclectic architectural style and historical significance make it a must-visit landmark.

Botong Twin Falls

Address: Osiao, Bacon District, Sorsogon City

Operating Hours: Open 24 hours

Description: Experience the unique charm of Botong Twin Falls, featuring two adjacent waterfalls with distinct blue and green hues. Located within the PNOC-BACMAN Geothermal Plant premises, these falls offer a serene spot for swimming and picnicking amidst lush greenery. The tranquil ambiance and natural beauty of the area provide a perfect escape for relaxation and nature appreciation.

Donsol

Whale Shark Interaction Center

Address: Donsol, Sorsogon

Operating Hours: 6:00 AM – 12:00 PM (Seasonal: November to June)

Dive into an extraordinary adventure in Donsol, the "Whale Shark Capital of the Philippines." Experience the thrill of swimming alongside gentle giants known as "butanding" in their natural habitat.

Bulusan

Bulusan Volcano Natural Park

Address: Bulusan, Sorsogon

Operating Hours: 6:00 AM – 5:00 PM

Embark on a journey through the lush landscapes of Bulusan Volcano Natural Park, a sanctuary encompassing Mount Bulusan, Bulusan Lake, and verdant rainforests. This protected area offers hiking trails, diverse wildlife, and serene picnic spots, providing a perfect escape for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. The park's breathtaking scenery and tranquil ambiance make it a highlight of any Sorsogon itinerary.

Bayugin Falls

Address: Barangay San Francisco, Bulusan, Sorsogon

Operating Hours: Open 24 hours

Discover the hidden gem of Bayugin Falls, nestled within a dense forest in Bulusan. This picturesque waterfall offers a refreshing retreat, with its cool waters cascading into a natural pool ideal for swimming. The journey to the falls involves a scenic hike through lush greenery, rewarding visitors with its pristine beauty and serene atmosphere—a perfect spot for nature lovers seeking tranquility.

Matnog

Subic Beach (Pink Sand Beach)

Address: Calintaan Island, Matnog, Sorsogon

Operating Hours: Open 24 hours

Escape to the enchanting shores of Subic Beach, renowned for its rare pinkish sand—a natural marvel resulting from crushed red corals blending with pristine white grains. This idyllic beach offers crystal-clear turquoise waters perfect for swimming, snorkeling, and unwinding. The tranquil surroundings make it ideal for beach camping or simply watching the sun set over the horizon. Whether you're seeking adventure or relaxation, Subic Beach promises an unforgettable getaway that belongs on every traveler's bucket list.

Gubat

Lola Sayong Eco-Surf Camp

Address: Barangay Buenavista, Gubat, Sorsogon

Operating Hours: 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Ride the waves at Lola Sayong Eco-Surf Camp, an eco-friendly destination that combines surfing lessons with environmental awareness. This surf camp offers a laid-back atmosphere, sustainable tourism practices, and a chance to connect with the local community. Whether you're a novice or experienced surfer, Lola Sayong provides an ideal setting for both adventure and relaxation.

Pilar

Juag Lagoon Marine Sanctuary

Address: Pilar, Sorsogon

Operating Hours: 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM

Explore the vibrant marine life at Juag Lagoon Marine Sanctuary, a privately-owned sanctuary that allows visitors to observe and interact with various marine species in their natural habitat. This educational experience promotes marine biodiversity conservation and offers a unique opportunity to learn about the rich aquatic ecosystem of Sorsogon.

Irosin

San Benon Hot Springs

Address: Barangay Monbon, Irosin, Sorsogon

Operating Hours: 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM

Unwind at San Benon Hot Springs, set against the backdrop of Mount Bulusan. These natural hot springs offer therapeutic waters ideal for relaxation and rejuvenation. The facility includes pools of varying temperatures amidst a lush, mountainous setting, providing a serene escape for those seeking tranquility and wellness.

Barcelona

Barcelona Church (St. Joseph Parish Church)

Address: Barcelona, Sorsogon

Operating Hours: 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Step back in time at Barcelona Church, a historic structure built during the Spanish colonial period. Showcasing classic architecture with coral stone walls, this church reflects the town's rich history and cultural legacy. Its serene ambiance and historical significance make it a must-visit for pilgrims and history enthusiasts alike.

Castilla

Balading Beach

Address: Barangay Balading, Castilla, Sorsogon

Operating Hours: Open 24 hours

Relax at Balading Beach, a serene coastal spot known for its calm waters and picturesque sunsets. This beach offers a peaceful environment for swimming, picnicking, and unwinding, making it a favorite among locals for weekend getaways. Its tranquil setting provides a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life.

Magallanes

Parola Beach and Lighthouse Station

Address: Barangay Parola, Magallanes, Sorsogon

Operating Hours: Open 24 hours

Discover the unspoiled beauty of Parola Beach, featuring fine white sand and clear waters ideal for swimming and beachcombing. The nearby lighthouse adds a historical touch to the scenic landscape, offering a picturesque backdrop for photography and exploration.

Juban

Juban Heritage Houses

Address: Juban, Sorsogon

Operating Hours: Exterior viewing available 24 hours; interior tours by appointment

Take a stroll through history in Juban, home to well-preserved ancestral houses showcasing Spanish-era architecture. These heritage homes reflect the town's rich history and cultural legacy, offering a glimpse into the past for history enthusiasts and cultural explorers.

Prieto Diaz

Mangrove Eco-Park

Address: Prieto Diaz, Sorsogon

Operating Hours: 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM

Immerse yourself in nature at Prieto Diaz Mangrove Ecopark, Sorsogon's top eco-tourism destination. Featuring 300 hectares of mangrove forest, 19 mangrove species, fish sanctuaries, and coral reefs, the park offers activities like bird watching, river cruises, and mangrove planting. It's an educational and recreational haven for nature lovers and environmental advocates.

Santa Magdalena

Talaonga Beach

Address: Barangay Talaonga, Santa Magdalena, Sorsogon

Operating Hours: 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM

Talaonga Beach is a tranquil coastal retreat nestled in the quiet municipality of Santa Magdalena, Sorsogon. Known for its scenic views of the Pacific, the beach features soft grayish sand, gentle waves, and a laid-back atmosphere that's perfect for swimming, relaxing, or enjoying a peaceful walk along the shore. With its secluded charm and unspoiled natural beauty, Talaonga Beach is a favorite among locals and off-the-beaten-path travelers seeking a serene escape away from busy tourist hubs.
