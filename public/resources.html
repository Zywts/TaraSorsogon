Travel Tips for Sorsogon

How to Get to Sorsogon

By Plane

The closest airport is in Legazpi, Albay. There are daily flights from Manila to Legazpi—book early for the best fares. From Legazpi, hop on a van or bus to Sorsogon City (≈2 hrs).

By Land

  • EDSA Cubao: Pintados (12:30 PM, ≈₱1 900), Isarog (3:30 PM, ≈₱1 340), Peñafrancia Tours (5:30 PM, ≈₱1 220), Elite (8:30 PM, ≈₱1 670).
  • LRT Buendia: DLTB (4:00 PM & 7:00 PM; ≈₱1 200).
  • Others from Pasay & Alabang; travel time ~14–16 hrs one way.

General Tips

  • Start Early: Beat the heat by heading out at dawn.
  • Stay Hydrated: Carry a refillable bottle.
  • Small Change: Keep coins/bills ready for fares.
  • Packing: Sunscreen, repellent, water shoes, rain jacket.
  • Etiquette: Dress modestly in churches; ask before photos.

Budget for Your Trip

A comfortable 3-day budget is ₱5 000–₱7 000, excluding transport fares.

Budget Tips

  • Join a larger whale-shark group to split boat/guide costs.
  • Stay in guesthouses or mid-range hotels.
  • Take jeepneys & UV-Express vans for inter-town travel.
  • Travel with a buddy to split lodging.
  • Eat at local carinderias for authentic, cheap meals.
